WorldRoamer

WorldRoamer® is an all-in-one online travel booking platform that enables travelers around the world to discover local destinations in Southeast Asia. With this hassle-free tool, travelers can book through hotel partners, explore local experiences, and browse resource guides to help plan their dream getaway.

Discover a new way to travel, with WorldRoamer®

Travel Inspiration

With a growing selection of travel options, WorldRoamer® makes it possible to create the ultimate dream vacation. Users can get inspired browsing the Travel Blog, full of natural attractions and cultural activities.

Book Easier & Faster

The consumer-facing website truly is a one-stop-shop full of useful features that streamline the booking process. Users can build and save custom itineraries or start with top-rated travel recommendations.

WorldRoamer® Partners

The Hotel Admin tool was created to bring added value to the many WorldRoamer® hospitality and accommodation partners, by offering competitive pricing, consumer trends, and live bookings.

Integrate With Your Team

WorldRoamer® approached us to create the technical solutions needed to develop and launch a new online travel agency. Our objective was to define a system architecture that is highly scaleable and extensible.

Microservices Model
Over 30 fullstack engineers used a new development workflow proven to increase the speed and efficiency of software solutions at scale.

Admin Portals
We developed key dashboards including the Activities and Hotel Admin portals, allowing the WorldRoamer® staff to archive and maintain customer details in the proper database.

Ease of Mind
With new systems in place, the finished website eliminates the hectic tasks customers and hoteliers formerly faced when searching for, and booking travel plans.

Launch a New Platform

The site will continue to update and improve user experience by working to understand traveler needs and provide the best experience possible.
