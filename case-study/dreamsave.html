DreamSave

DreamSave™ is a new fintech mobile app making it easy for women in developing countries realize their dreams of a better life and break the cycle of poverty.

The Story

DreamSave™ approached AgilityIO with a goal to incorporate the latest behavioral science research to help informal community banks and saving groups manage financial records, connect securely to formal financial services, and build verified credit scores.

User Experience

DreamStart Labs and AgilityIO implemented a human-centered design methodology called Rapid Prototyping - all product design begins with a deep understanding of the goals and needs of end users while using restraint in defining requirements.

Each new product sprint is designed to validate assumptions with end users at every step of the way, a process that is crucial in order to maximize the likelihood of product success.

App Design & Features

Simplify Meetings
Through eliminating tedious micro-tasks, DreamSave allows your team to focus on what is most important. Managing savings groups has never been simpler; by utilizing a smooth user experience, Dreamsave has found a way to make even the most complex meetings simple.

Achieve Goals Faster
Using the latest research in behavioral science, DreamSave has incorporated several tools to help members achieve their personal and financial goals. Through gamification and predictive analyses, DreamSave has made financial literacy the norm.

Automate Transactions
No more complexity. No more errors. No more debates. DreamSaves' automated system strives to make financial transactions as intuitive as they should be.

The Result

Working with Project Concern International, DreamSave is first targeting women in village savings and loan groups, working with them to save towards a central investment fund, which is then used for business loans to members. Unlike outside bank loans, all interest payments go back to the group, so when one member's business grows everyone benefits.
