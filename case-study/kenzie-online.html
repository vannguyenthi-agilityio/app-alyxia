Kenzie Online

Founded by Silicon Valley tech entrepreneurs, Kenzie Academy is a tech apprenticeship school designed and taught by industry practitioners cultivating the next generation of tech talent.

Kenzie Academy offers online and on-campus programs for students throughout the US and Brazil. Software Engineering covers fullstack development including: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL, React, Python, and Django. UX Engineering covers user-driven design with frontend coding.

Kenzie Free is a series of free, online mini-courses for users to try coding at their own pace.

On-Demand Courses

Students can manage their profile, access coursework anywhere and learn how to code from the comfort of their home.

Enhanced Skillsets

The program is designed by industry experts from Google, LinkedIn, and Zillow. Kenzie's Employer Partner Network advises on course curriculum, so students can learn highly marketable, relevant skills.

For Beginners or Refreshers

Kenzie Free is educational for both beginners and professionals seeking to upskill their potential. The platform features video tutorials and coding practice sessions with real-time analytical results.
